About Us

Greenfield Public School is a co-educational day school offering classes from Kindergarten to Grade 12. We focus on balanced development — academics, sports, arts and life-skills.

Experienced Teachers

Qualified, caring educators who focus on each student's growth.

Holistic Curriculum

A blend of academics and co-curricular activities to foster creativity.

Safe Environment

Secure campus, student counseling, and a supportive community.

Academics & Programs

We offer a broad set of programs including STEM, Languages, Fine Arts, Sports and Special Education support.

Primary Years

Foundation-level literacy and numeracy with activity-based learning.

Secondary

Subject specialization, exam preparation and career guidance.

After-school Clubs

Robotics, Debate, Music, Art and Sports coaching.
